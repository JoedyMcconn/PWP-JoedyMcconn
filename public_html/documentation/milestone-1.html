Purpose, Audience, Goal

Purpose:The purpose of this website is to create a portfolio webpage to showcase my HTML and CSS styling skills.

Audience:The website is primarily designed for Professional peers and developers Potential Employers Educators and Mentors Friends, Tech enthusiasts, Learners.

Goal:The single focused goal driving the creation of this portfolio is to eventually one day obtain a job because of these skills.

Persona

Name:Ron Huckleback

Profession:Founder and owner of AnyWayz, a new startup looking for a full-time hybrid backend web developers. Their product is a map that updates live to give the best route possible while including live traffic.

Technology:Strict Windows user. Everything Ron has learned and been taught has been involved with a Windows PC. He uses three monitors and a desktop as his main way of using the internet but also has a samsung s21 ultra.

Attitudes & Behaviors: Ron is desperately looking for qualified candidates to hire but due to being a newly founded business owner, finds himself short on time. Ron only has about 45 minutes a day to look for potential workers for a very important position, he is actively seeking candidates through job boards, and community tech events around his area.

Frustrations:Ron is too short on time to do a traditional interview process with dozens of potential hires, he is looking for experienced and entry-level coders as his current startup is still in it's beginning stages. Ron realizes reading through resumes and cover letters just does not give him a full scope of a person's identity as he is looking for someone to develop and stay within the company for a few years at least.

Goals:Ron has only one interest and that is to hire people to fill his back end developer positions. He is faced with a challenge as many business owners are and that is being short on time. He needs to know if this person is worthy of being interviewed as he is never able to tell from resume's and cover letters alone.

User Story:As a visitor, I need to make a choice if the person who made this portfolio deserves an interview for a position.
